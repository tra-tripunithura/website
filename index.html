About Us

Thamaramkulangara Residence Association (TRA) was formed in 2019 by residents of the Thamaramkulangara neighborhood, adjacent to the historic Thamaramkulangara Sree Dharmasastha Temple in Tripunithura, Ernakulam

The Thamaramkulangara Residence Association (TRA) is a community-driven organization dedicated to fostering a spirit of cooperation, support, and development for the residents of Thamaramkulangara. Our mission is to create a harmonious living environment through collective efforts, effective communication, and mutual respect among all members of the association.

Objective

Community Engagement : Organise social and cultural events for residents to come together and build lasting relationships.

Safety and Security : Enhance the safety and security of our neighbourhood through joint initiatives and vigilance.

Environmental Sustainability : Promote eco-friendly practices and maintain green spaces for the well-being of our community.

Infrastructure Development : Collaborate with local authorities to improve and maintain infrastructure like roads, water supply, and waste management.
